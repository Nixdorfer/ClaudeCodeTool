## Design Rules

- all shapes rounded min-radius 12px preferred 16-24px
- colors soft avoid high-saturation harsh tones
- use cute emoji decorations generously
- shadows soft use pink-tinted shadows not pure black
- animations lively bouncy floaty spinny
- text warm friendly tone
- no sharp corners no dark heavy colors
