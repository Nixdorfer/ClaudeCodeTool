| 强调色 | 5 | accent-primary accent-secondary accent-tertiary accent-highlight accent-quaternary |

| 输入组件 | 9 | input-bg input-border input-text input-placeholder input-focus-border input-focus-shadow input-disabled-bg input-disabled-text input-selection |
| 滚动条 | 3 | scrollbar-track scrollbar-thumb scrollbar-thumb-hover |
| 阴影 | 4+2 | shadow-sm shadow-md shadow-lg shadow-xl shadow-btn shadow-btn-hover |
